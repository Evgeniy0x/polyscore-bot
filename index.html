Security

Your keys. Your funds.
Protected by design.

Non-custodial architecture with AES-256 encryption and self-custody option.

  • AES-256 Encrypted Keys
    Private keys encrypted with AES-256 + HMAC-SHA256 before storage. Even if the database is compromised, keys are unusable without the separate master key.
  • Cloud-Isolated Master Key
    The encryption master key lives exclusively in cloud environment variables. An attacker would need both database and cloud access simultaneously.
  • Self-Custody Option
    Connect your own MetaMask or Polygon wallet. In self-custody mode, your private key never touches our infrastructure.
  • On-Chain Settlement
    All trades settle on Polymarket's smart contracts on Polygon. Funds go directly to your wallet when a market resolves.
